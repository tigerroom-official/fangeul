## Full Description
Fangeul — Korean tools made for K-pop fans

🔤 Hangul Keyboard
Type in Korean without installing a Korean keyboard.
An intuitive on-screen keypad that anyone can pick up instantly.

🔄 English ↔ Hangul Converter
Type "gksrmf" on an English keyboard and get "한글" instantly!
You can also reverse Korean text back to English key layout.

🗣 Korean Pronunciation (Romanizer)
See the accurate romanized pronunciation of any Korean text.
Covers 7 pronunciation rules including liaison, nasalization, and aspiration.

💬 K-pop Fan Phrases
Find the perfect Korean message for your favorite idol.
Birthday wishes, cheering, love confessions, fandom phrases — copy and use right away!
Available in 8 languages: Korean, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Japanese.

🫧 Floating Bubble
A mini converter that floats over other apps.
Convert and copy Hangul while using Weverse, Bubble, or social media.

🎨 Theme Customization
Decorate the app with your own colors.
Express your fan identity with the colors you love.
